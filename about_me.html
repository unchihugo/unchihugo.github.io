I'm Hugo Li, I'm 19 and I am currently studying in the UK for my Bachelor's degree. I was born in the Netherlands, and moved to Belgium at six. I am from Chinese heritage :)

When I was little, my father owned an electronics repair shop. He often brought me to his humble shop, and I was fascinated by all the technology that came in: from the first iPhone to a Nintendo DS to a Brother printer ‚Äî he fixed it. Him being in the space of technology, I soon got my first devices to play with. It is safe to say that my father kick started my interest in technology at a very young age.

In elementary school, most kids get their first phone. I didn't, but I did get a laptop instead! Playing games after school really intrigued me with the idea of making my own games. I started with modelling. With the power of the internet, people were there along the way to teach me new things, such as modelling techniques and graphics art. Soon enough, I created story, showcase and platformer games (they weren't good). There was one thing though: I could not figure out how to program for the life of me, but at 14, I had my first programming class. It's safe to say that I excelled, and I was absolutely hooked.

Nowadays I am a freelancer and have previously worked & am working with several teams as a programmer or designer. I have also interned for software companies in-person. I know how to work alone and in a team and to quickly learn theory, ideas, and methods to effectively fulfill job responsibilities.

Programming and designing/arts are my passions, and I love to work on those skills in my pastime.
